About Me

Who I Am

I'm Mpho Dynal Mabena, an IT professional based in Johannesburg, South Africa. My journey in technology began with a curiosity about how systems work together — from the code that powers web applications to the servers that host them. I believe in building solutions that are not only functional but also secure, maintainable, and user-friendly.

My Approach

I view technology as a tool to solve real-world problems. Whether I'm designing a database schema, troubleshooting a server issue, or developing a RESTful API, I focus on the bigger picture: how does this serve the end user? I'm drawn to full-stack development because it allows me to understand and contribute to every layer of an application — from the browser down to the operating system.

In my practical experience, I've learned that the best solutions come from clear documentation, thorough testing, and a willingness to dive into logs to find the root cause of an issue. I enjoy the detective work that comes with troubleshooting, and I take pride in writing code and configurations that others can easily understand and maintain.

Technical Journey & Interests

My formal education at Vaal University of Technology gave me a strong foundation in IT principles, but my real growth has come from hands-on projects. I'm particularly passionate about:

  • Linux System Administration: Managing CentOS servers, hardening security, and understanding the intricacies of user permissions and firewall configurations.
  • Web Development: Building responsive frontends with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and creating robust backends with Python (Flask) and Node.js.
  • Database Design: Crafting efficient schemas in MySQL/MariaDB and optimizing queries for performance.
  • DevOps Practices: Deploying applications, monitoring logs, and managing the development-to-production workflow.
  • Security Hardening: Implementing firewall configurations, user permissions, and security patches to protect applications and data.

Beyond the Code

When I'm not working on servers or writing code, I enjoy exploring new technologies, contributing to technical documentation, and collaborating with others to solve challenging problems. I'm a firm believer in continuous learning — which is why I've pursued certifications in AI and work readiness through the YES programme, even after completing my diploma.

I'm currently open to opportunities that allow me to apply my skills in full-stack development, IT infrastructure, or system administration, and I'm always excited to connect with like-minded professionals.
